My Recipe Book

A collection of delicious recipes

Butter Chicken

Creamy and flavorful chicken curry made with butter, cream, and spices.

Ingredients: Chicken, butter, cream, spices

Instructions: Cook chicken with spices, add butter and cream, simmer until thickened.

Pani Puri

Small, round crispy puris filled with spicy tamarind water, chickpeas, potatoes, and spices.

Ingredients: Puri shells, tamarind water, chickpeas, potatoes, spices

Instructions: Fill puris with chickpeas, potatoes, pour tamarind water, and serve immediately.

Masala Dosa

Thin and crispy rice crepe filled with spiced potatoes served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, potatoes, spices

Instructions: Ferment rice and lentil batter, spread thin on griddle, fill with spiced potatoes, serve with chutney and sambar.

Gulab Jamun

Soft and spongy milk solids dumplings soaked in flavored sugar syrup.

Ingredients: Milk solids, sugar, cardamom

Instructions: Make dough with milk solids, fry until golden, soak in sugar syrup.

Chole Bhature

Spicy chickpea curry served with fluffy fried bread (bhature).

Ingredients: Chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, spices, flour, yogurt

Instructions: Cook chickpeas with spices, make dough for bhature, fry until puffed and golden.

Samosa Chaat

Crunchy samosas topped with tangy tamarind chutney, yogurt, and spices.

Ingredients: Potatoes, peas, flour, spices, tamarind chutney, yogurt

Instructions: Make samosas with spiced potato filling, fry until crispy, top with chutney and yogurt.
